MonyetSpeak

National Taiwan University
March 2024 - June 2024

MonyetSpeak leverages generative AI to preserve Malaysian 'Rojak' English: a code-switched blend of English, Malay, and more in informal digital communication.

  • Fine-tuned a GPT-2 model using Hugging Face libraries and Google Colab to generate Malaysian 'Rojak' English from tokenized subreddit data
  • Utilized PRAW for web scraping and Pandas for data cleaning and tokenization
  • Evaluated model performance using metrics for fluency, coherence, and adherence to Malaysian English through Likert scale quantitative analysis with native speakers
